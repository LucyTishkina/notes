Copper connectors


RJ11 (register jack 11) connector
- 6 position, 2 conductor (6P2C)
→ RJ14 uses 6P4C for dual-line use
- Telephone connection

RJ45-connector
- 8 position, 8 conductor (8P8C)
→ modular connector

BNC connector
- Bayonet Neill-Concelman
→ Paul Neill (Bell Labs) and Carl Concelman (Amphenol)
- Coaxial cable connector
→ Used in DS3 WAN links
- Rigid and bulky
→ Can be difficult to work with

DB-9 and DB-25
- D-subminiature or D-sub
→ Different sizes, A through E
- Commonly used for RS-232
→ Recommended Standard 232
→ An industry standard since 1969
- Serial communications standard
→ Build for modem communication
⇒ used for modems, printers, mice, networking
- Commonly used as a configuration port
→ serial console interface

F-connector
- Cable television
→ cable modem
- RG-6 cable
→ threaded connector
