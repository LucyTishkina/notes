Recovery Sites


Cold site
- no hardware
→ empty building
- no data
→ bring it with you
- no people
→ bus in your team

Warm site
- somewhere between cold and hot
→ just enough to get going
- big room with rack space
→ you bring the hardware
- hardware is ready and waiting
→ you bring the software and data

Hot site
- an exact replica
→ duplicate everything
- stocked with hardware
→ constantly updated
→ you buy 2 of everything
→ an expensive recovery option
- applications and software are constantly updated
→ automated replication
- flip a switch and everything moves
→ this may be quite a few switches
