Optical Fiber Connectors


ST Connector
- Straight tip
→ Bayonet connector
- push in and twist, untwist and pull when connecting in/out of equipment

SC connector
- Subscriber connector
- Standard connector
- Square connector
- push in and pull out when connecting in/out of equipment

LC
- Lucent Connector
- Local Connector
- “Little Connector”
- locking mechanism

MT-RJ
- mechanical transfer registered jack
- media termination - recommended jack
- smaller than the other connectors
